Annual Procurement Plan

The APP is the document that consolidates the various Project Procurement Management Plan (PPMP) submitted by the various Project Management Offices (PMI) and end-user units within the Procuring Entity. It reflects the entirety of the procurement activities that will be undertaken by the Procuring Entity within the calendar year.
